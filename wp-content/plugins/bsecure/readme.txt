bSecure - Your Universal Checkout

== Description ==

bSecure is a universal checkout made for the Pakistan market that is easy & simple to install on any WooCommerce store. It gives you an option to enable one-step check out and accept multiple payment method for your customers, as well as run your business hassle free. You can create your account on [bSecure](https://partner.bsecure.pk/signup) for free and our team will guide you along. 
By following a few simple steps, you can be up and running in no time. Leave the rest to us. We will take care of the login, address, shipping methods and payments on your behalf. All you must do is configure your checkout once on the bSecure Partner portal once and leave the rest to us.
bSecure is built for desktop, tablet, and mobile devices. It is continuously tested and updated to offer a frictionless payment experience, we have integrated with a few of your favorite payment methods and many more to follow. Some of them include: 

COD (Cash on delivery)
Credit/Debit Card (Live)
EasyPaisa Wallet (Live)
EasyPaisa OTC (Over the counter) (Live)
Bank transfer (Live)
NIFT Payment Gateway (Coming Soon)
APPS payment gateway (Coming Soon)
HBL Konnect (Coming Soon)
HBL Pay (Coming Soon)
HBL CyberSource (Coming Soon)
JazzCash (Coming Soon)
1Link (Coming Soon)

You can do much more with bSecure and enhance your customer’s overall experience. You can:

Enable multiple payment methods (COD, Bank Alfalah, Easy Paisa, etc.) on our universal platform.
Automate your confirmation calls with our Automated Calling feature, it will allow you to reduce doorstep refusal and increase your COD conversion Rate. 
Offer voucher or cart value-based discounts to your customers
Increase your conversion rate with our seamless checkout solution
Customize your cart's minimum order value
Communicate in your customer's language
Generate QR codes, which will redirect your customers to the checkout page. 
Create manual orders or manage your product catalog with bSecure 
Design your own branded checkout and turn on SMS/Email notifications as well!
Manage multiple shipping methods

We will soon bring in and help you integrate with your favorite logistics and fulfillment providers including Trax, TCS, Bykea, Movex, DHL, Leopards and many more courier services in Pakistan.  


Remember to create your account at [bSecure](https://partner.bsecure.pk/signup) and our support team will guide you in case of any queries.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Can I receive payment on mobile? =

Yes. bSecure  is optimized for mobile and automatically shows all payment method if enabled

= What is maximum limit of amount that can be received per order? =

There is no payment limitation against the order

== Installation ==

The easiest way to install WP bSecure plugin is to search for it on your site’s Dashboard.

STEP-BY-STEP INSTRUCTIONS

Log in to your site’s dashboard (e.g. www.yourdomain.com/wp-admin).
Click on the “Plugins” tab in the left panel, then click “Add New”.
Search for “bSecure” and find the plugin at the top.
Install it by clicking the “Install Now” link.
When installation finishes, click “Activate Plugin”.
Go to the settings section of bSecure within the WooCommerce Settings page
Add in the API Key & Credentials from your [bSecure account](https://partner.bsecure.pk/integration-live)
